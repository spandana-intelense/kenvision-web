About Intelense

Intelense is an AI technology company building real-time intelligence platforms that transform video, sensor, and machine data into actionable insights. Founded with a focus on computer vision and AI systems, Intelense helps organizations move from passive data collection to proactive decision-making.

Our platforms integrate seamlessly with existing infrastructure and are designed to scale across industries, environments, and geographies.

Visit Intelense.com

Ken360 — The Intelligence Engine

Ken360 is Intelense's core AI intelligence engine that powers all Ken products. It acts as a unified learning and decision layer, processing inputs from cameras, sensors, and systems to detect patterns, identify anomalies, and generate actionable insights in real time.

Ken360 enables:

  • ✓ Multi-modal data fusion
  • ✓ Context-aware intelligence
  • ✓ Adaptive learning over time
  • ✓ Reliable, high-confidence insights

Ken360 is the foundation that allows each Ken product to deliver industry-specific intelligence without rebuilding AI models from scratch.
